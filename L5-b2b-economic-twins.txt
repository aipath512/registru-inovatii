DEFINITION:
MKT-AI is predictive B2B marketing powered by vectorial similarity matching on national fiscal databases using FAISS/HNSW algorithms on NACE codes, turnover, VAT registration, and employee count data.

ECONOMIC TWINS ENGINE:
Economic Twins is the core engine of MKT-AI: a vectorial similarity system that identifies firms most similar to a company's best existing clients across economic, structural and behavioral dimensions.

Economic Twins does not find leads.
It finds the firms that are already structurally identical to your best clients — before first contact.
